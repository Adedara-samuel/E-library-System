Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download a book?

To download a book, navigate to the course page and click on the download link provided for each book.

How do I change my password?

You can change your password by visiting the Profile section and selecting the 'Change Password' option.

Are there any video tutorials available?

Yes, we provide video tutorials for various courses. You can find them on the Course page under each course description.

How can I contact support?

For any support-related queries, please send an email to the department admin or locate the adminblock.
